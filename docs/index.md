## 📋 Chapter Breakdown

### [Chapter 1: Solution of Non-Linear Equations](ch1)

Find roots of functions using both bracketing and open methods.

**Topics:**
- Errors and Accuracy in Numerical Computations
- Bisection Method
- Regula Falsi & Secant Methods
- Newton-Raphson Method
- Fixed-Point Iteration
- Systems of Non-Linear Equations (with Jacobian matrices)

### [Chapter 2: System of Linear Equations](ch2)

Solve systems of linear algebraic equations using direct and iterative methods.

**Topics:**
- Gaussian Elimination & Pivoting Strategies
- Gauss-Jordan Method
- Matrix Inverse using Factorization
- LU Decomposition (Doolittle & Crout Methods)
- Jacobi & Gauss-Seidel Iterative Methods
- Eigenvalues & Eigenvectors (Power Method)

### [Chapter 3: Interpolation](ch3)

Approximate functions using interpolation techniques and finite differences.

**Topics:**
- Uniqueness of Interpolation Polynomials
- Lagrange's Interpolation
- Newton's Divided Difference Method
- Finite Differences (Forward, Backward, Central)
- Newton-Gregory Formulas
- Stirling's & Bessel's Central Difference Interpolation
